This is a followup project of Project 1. For data science project, it is important to visualize the data to better understand the problem. In this project, an interactive visualization website was built using Dash and Plotly. You can select arbitry camera pair including a left camera and a right camera among eight cameras. Then, by selecting a bubble image in left camera, the candidate bubble images in right camera will be highlighted. Plus, the prediction results of the candidate particles are presented with classifiers trained in Project 1.

This is a project link hosted on Heroku: https://vptv-two-camera-correspondence.herokuapp.com/. Note that the first time you opened this link might take 30 seconds.

The interface is shown below:

  • Dropdown 1: selects the index of left camera.
  • Dropdown 2: selects the index of right camera.
  • Dropdown 3: selects the index of particle in left camera.
  • Slider 1: scale down the image resolution in case they cannot fit in page.
  • Left image: image of left camera.
  • Right image: image of right camera.
  • Purple circles: detected particles, where orange dot indicates currently selected particle.
  • Gold circles: particle candidates in right camera.
  • Orange annotation:
    • log_label: the predicted label of logistic regression classifier.
    • tree_label: the predicted label of decision tree classifier.
    • nn_label: the predicted label of neural network classifier.
